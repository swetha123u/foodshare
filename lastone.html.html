About Us

Food wastage is a major challenge in restaurants, hostels, and community kitchens due to unpredictable demand and poor inventory tracking. Traditional food ordering systems only focus on order placement but fail to address overproduction, unsold items, and community needs. There is a need for a smart food ordering system that not only predicts demand but also minimizes waste by enabling community sharing of surplus food.

  • Predictive Inventory Management: Uses historical order data to forecast demand and guide food preparation.
  • Community Sharing Module: Surplus food can be shared/donated to nearby users, NGOs, or communities in real-time.
  • Zero-Waste Goal: By aligning preparation with demand and redistributing leftovers, the system minimizes food wastage.
  • Integration of Ordering & Sustainability: Combines regular customer food ordering with waste-reduction initiatives in one platform.
  • First system to merge food ordering + prediction + waste reduction in one platform.
  • Introduces community sharing of surplus food.
  • Helps restaurants save costs & promotes sustainability.
  • Provides analytics dashboard for admins to track waste reduction progress.
